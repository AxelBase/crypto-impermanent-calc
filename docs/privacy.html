Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Crypto Impermanent Loss Calculator, a free, open-source web utility that helps users understand impermanent loss in decentralized liquidity pools. This tool operates entirely in your browser and is built with a strong commitment to privacy by design. We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices clearly so you can use the calculator with complete confidence.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The calculator is designed to function without gathering any information from users. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): No names, email addresses, wallet addresses, or any identifying details.
  • Usage or Behavioral Data: No IP addresses, browser fingerprints, device information, or location data.
  • Input Data: All price ratios and calculations you enter are processed locally in your browser and are never sent to any server.
  • Analytics or Tracking Data: We do not use cookies, analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics), pixels, or any tracking scripts.

Because everything runs client-side using JavaScript, your inputs and results remain exclusively on your device.

3. How the Tool Works

When you use the Crypto Impermanent Loss Calculator:

  • Local Processing Only: Price ratio inputs are immediately used by JavaScript to compute impermanent loss using standard mathematical formulas. No data leaves your browser.
  • No Server Interaction: The site is a static application. There is no backend, database, or API that receives user data.
  • Temporary Session Data: Any values stored temporarily (e.g., form inputs during a session) exist only in browser memory and are cleared on page refresh or closure.

This architecture ensures absolute data isolation and makes the tool safe for exploring even sensitive trading scenarios.

4. Third-Party Services

We use only essential, privacy-respecting third-party CDNs for Bootstrap styling and icons. These resources do not enable tracking or data collection about individual users. No advertising, social media, or analytics services are integrated.

5. Cookies and Tracking

This site does not use cookies, localStorage for persistence, sessionStorage beyond immediate use, or any other tracking technologies. Your experience is completely stateless.

6. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored by us, the primary responsibility for security lies with your device and browser. We recommend using up-to-date browsers and secure connections (HTTPS) when accessing the tool.

7. Children's Privacy

The calculator is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue on our public GitHub repository.

Your privacy is our priority. By using this tool, you acknowledge our zero-data-collection approach.
